Privacy Policy – Dneato Rider Partner

Dneato Rider Partner app is used by delivery riders to manage orders and earnings.

Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Live location for delivery tracking
  • KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License, RC)
  • Order, delivery, and earnings data
  • Device information and push notification token

How We Use Information

  • To assign and manage delivery orders
  • To verify rider identity (KYC)
  • To calculate and process earnings
  • To send order notifications

Data Security

All data is securely stored using Firebase services and protected against unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase services for authentication, database, and storage.

User Rights

Users can request data deletion or account removal by contacting us.
